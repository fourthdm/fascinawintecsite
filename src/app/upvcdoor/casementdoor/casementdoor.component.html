Casement Door





Product Overview


The casement door is an open able door with or without flying mullion. This can be offered in a combination of fixed and open able.

uPVC Casement Door Single Open is very good option for application Residential Home, Row houses, Bunglow, Commercial corporate offices, Hospitals, Colleges and High-Rise Buildings. This uPVC door can be fitted with Glass: 4 – 36 mm of Annealed / Tinted / Toughened / Frosted / Single Glaze / Double Glaze and laminated. Laminated. Multi-point Locking System gives extra safety, 3D Hinges and Projection Handle for smooth movement.

  • Multi-point locking system: Projection Handle with key
  • 3 Chamber Sash / 3 Chamber Outer Frame – more chambers
  • more resistance to thermal conductivity
  • Smooth movement: 3 D hinges
  • Double sealing by EPDM gasket to reduce sound
  • Arrest air leakages Seamless weld joints
