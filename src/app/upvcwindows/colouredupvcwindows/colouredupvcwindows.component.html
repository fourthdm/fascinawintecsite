Coloured Windows





Product Overview


We can also offer windows made with special weather resistant coloured laminations. The coloured laminations are imported from Germany.

They are equipped to deal with tropical weather and come with a 20 year warranty. Wooden textures have always been an evergreen choice when it comes to beautifying our properties. In a bid to preserve nature, wooden textured laminates are a perfect replacement. Prominance houses a wide range of wood grain textured laminates in Mahogany, Light Oak, Walnut and more.


  • Laminates are equipped with PVDF
  • Extreme weather resistance.
  • Crack resistance.
  • Colour stability
  • Gloss retention
  • Easy maintenance
  • Environment-friendly (Imported PUR base Adhesive)
