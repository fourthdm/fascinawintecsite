Casement Windows





Product Overview


uPVC casement windows also called a side hung window which opens inside or outside. The top hung is a window which opens from bottom to top. The above can also offered in a combination of fixed and open able windows. The sash is connected to the outer frame by means of friction hinges or butt hinges.
